Background-Origin: CSS3 specific

This box has no background-origin set. Hence the background image stays within the borders i.e. within the padding-box by default.
background-origin: border-box. Hence the background image starts from the upper left corner of the border.
background-origin: content-box. Hence the background image starts from the upper left corner of the content.

Background-Clip: CSS3 specific

No background-clip set. Background clipped to border-box by default.
background-clip: padding-box. Background clipped to padding-box.
background-clip: content-box. Background clipped to content-box.

Background-Attachment

scroll local fixed

Scroll: background scrolls along with the element by default

Local: background scrolls along with the element's contents

Fixed: background is fixed with regard to the viewport
